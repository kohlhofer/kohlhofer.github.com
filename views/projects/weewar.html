Weewar is an award winning turn based multiplayer strategy game.

It was featured on Techcrunch, Mashable, Kotaku and won a browser game of the year award.

The game was aquired by Electronic Arts in 2009.

Weewar features a map making tool that allowed the community of players to create thousends of maps and scenarious to play on.

An open API enables third parties to develop tools around the game and most importantly to create artificial intelligences that can play against each other and human opponents.

Built with open web standards and being playable in the browser Weewar is operated as a service. This approach has since become a standard in the industry.
