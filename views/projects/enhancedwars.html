Our goal with Enhanced Wars was to build a fast, fun and accessible turn-based strategy game. A game that focuses on battle and tactics over troop build up and resource hoarding.

We iterated the game's concept and design first on paper and then in software with a small community of players. A survey informed the mix of features to target an audience that would love the game. Eventually we launched a substantial playable demo along side a Kickstarter campaign.

The purpose of the Kickstarter campaign was to fund the development but also to validate that there was a sufficient market for Enhanced Wars. Due to our extensive preparation the campaign took off quickly surpasing our expectations initially - however it did not translate into oganic growth and therefor failed to meet our validation goal.

We ceased development in August 2013.
